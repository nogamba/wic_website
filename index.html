About Us

We're coders and engineers - both women and men - who support the female presence in computing. We run events, from tech talks to socials, and provide opportunities for female engineers to connect and succeed.

Technical Events

We have industry professionals come hold tech talks, including all-female engineer panels. Bring your resumes!

Learn More

Grad School Preparation

We hold events and panels that give students resources for planning the next steps in their educational career.

Learn More

Conference Scholarships

WIC attends annual conferences in the Southern California region and also sponsors girls to attend the national Grace Hopper Conference.

Learn More

Coding Competitions

Every quarter, we hold a beginner's programming competition, restricted to students who have yet to take upper division classes.
